Suppose that a is 3.14159. What do each of these statements write? 

A.stdio.writeln(a)
B.stdio.writeln(a + 1.0)
C.stdio.writeln(8 // int(a))
D.stdio.writeln(8.0 / a)
E.stdio.writeln(int(8.0 / a))


Ans. Ex. 1- 2 - 8

stdio.writeln(a) -> 3.14159
stdio.writeln(a + 1.0) -> 4.14159
stdio.writeln(8 // int(a)) -> 2
stdio.writeln(8.0 / a) -> 2.5464812403910124
stdio.writeln(int(8.0 / a)) -> 2
